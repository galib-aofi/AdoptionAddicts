Your Journey to Pet Parenthood Starts Here

At Adodoption Addict, we've designed our adoption process to ensure the best possible matches between pets and their new families. Our 5-step process helps us understand your needs while ensuring our animals find safe, loving forever homes.

The 5-Step Adoption Process

  1. Browse Available Pets
  2. Submit Application
  3. Meet & Greet Session
  4. Home Safety Check
  5. Finalize Adoption

Average Processing Time: 3-7 days

Adoption Fee Range: $50-$300 (depending on animal type and age)

Step 1: Browse Available Pets

What You'll Do:

  • Browse our online database of available animals
  • Visit our adoption center during open hours
  • Read detailed profiles of each animal
  • Note down animals you're interested in meeting

Tips for This Step:

  • Keep an open mind - sometimes the perfect pet isn't what you initially imagined
  • Consider your lifestyle, living space, and experience level
  • Read the entire animal profile, including medical and behavioral notes
  • Make a list of 2-3 animals you'd like to meet

Step 2: Submit Your Application

What You'll Do:

  • Complete our online adoption application form
  • Provide personal and veterinary references
  • Answer questions about your pet experience and lifestyle
  • Specify which animals you're interested in

Application Requirements:

  • Must be 21 years or older
  • Provide proof of address
  • Show identification
  • If renting, provide landlord approval for pets
  • All household members should be involved in the decision

What We Look For:

  • Compatibility between your lifestyle and the animal's needs
  • Understanding of the responsibilities of pet ownership
  • Financial ability to care for the animal
  • Safe and appropriate living environment

Step 3: Meet & Greet Session

What You'll Do:

  • Schedule a meeting with the animal(s) you're interested in
  • Interact with the animal in our designated meeting areas
  • Ask our staff questions about the animal's behavior and needs
  • Bring all family members (including current pets if appropriate)

During the Meeting:

  • Observe the animal's personality and behavior
  • Discuss any concerns with our adoption counselors
  • Learn about the animal's routine, diet, and medical history
  • For dogs: We may conduct a dog-to-dog introduction if you have other pets

What to Bring:

  • All decision-making family members
  • Questions you have for our staff
  • For dog meetings: your current dog(s) for compatibility testing
  • Any necessary documentation

Step 4: Home Safety Check

What This Involves:

  • Virtual home tour or in-person visit (depending on location)
  • Assessment of safety hazards
  • Verification of adequate space for the animal
  • Discussion of preparation needed before adoption

We Check For:

  • Secure fencing (for dogs)
  • Safe spaces for the animal to eat, sleep, and play
  • Absence of obvious hazards
  • Appropriate living conditions

This is NOT:

  • A white-glove cleanliness inspection
  • A judgment of your decorating style
  • An income verification

We're simply ensuring the animal will be safe and comfortable in your home.

Step 5: Finalize Adoption

What You'll Do:

  • Sign adoption agreement and contracts
  • Pay adoption fee
  • Receive medical records and care information
  • Schedule pickup or take your new pet home

Adoption Fee Includes:

  • Spay/neuter surgery
  • Initial vaccinations
  • Microchipping
  • Health check-up
  • Starter kit (food samples, care guide, etc.)

Post-Adoption Support:

  • 30-day follow-up check-in
  • Access to our behavior helpline
  • Discounted training classes
  • Veterinary referral network
  • Online community of adopters

Congratulations!

You're now part of the Adodoption Addict family! We're here to support you throughout your pet's life.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire process take?

Typically 3-7 days, depending on application volume and scheduling availability.

What if I change my mind after adoption?

We have a 14-day trial period. If the adoption isn't working out, we'll help find a better match.

Can I put an animal on hold?

Yes, with an approved application, we can hold an animal for 24 hours.

Do you offer same-day adoptions?

Rarely. We believe in thoughtful matches, but exceptions may be made for pre-approved applicants.

What if my application is denied?

We'll explain why and may suggest alternative animals or help you prepare for future adoption.
