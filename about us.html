Our Mission: Creating Forever Connections

At Adoption Addict, we believe every pet deserves a loving home and every home deserves the joy of a pet. Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small community initiative to a nationwide network of animal lovers dedicated to making perfect pet matches.

We're not just about finding homes for pets - we're about creating lasting relationships that enrich both human and animal lives. Our name says it all: we're addicted to seeing the happiness that comes from successful adoptions!

What We Do

Adoption Addict serves as the bridge between loving homes and pets in need. Our comprehensive platform offers:

  • Pet Matching Service: Our unique algorithm helps match your lifestyle with the perfect pet personality
  • Adoption Portal: Browse hundreds of pets from shelters and rescue organizations nationwide
  • Post-Adoption Support: Resources, training tips, and community support for new pet parents
  • Educational Resources: Comprehensive guides on pet care, behavior, and health
  • Community Events: Adoption fairs, training workshops, and pet-friendly gatherings

Find Your Perfect Match

We work with all types of pets to help you find your ideal companion:

Dogs

From energetic puppies to calm seniors, small breeds to gentle giants. Whether you're looking for a running partner or a couch cuddler, we have dogs of all ages, sizes, and energy levels waiting for their forever homes.

Cats

Playful kittens, dignified adults, lap cats and independent spirits. Our feline friends come in all personalities and coat types, including special needs cats who need extra love and care.

Small Animals

Rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters, rats, and other small companions. These often-overlooked pets make wonderful additions to homes with limited space or specific allergy concerns.

Birds

From cheerful parakeets to intelligent parrots, we help place feathered friends in appropriate homes where they can thrive with proper care and attention.

Reptiles & Special Species

For experienced owners, we occasionally have reptiles, amphibians, and other unique pets needing rehoming with knowledgeable caretakers.

Our Unique Post-Adoption Program

Adoption is just the beginning! Our support continues long after you bring your new friend home:

  • 30-Day Adjustment Support: Dedicated hotline for new pet parents with questions or concerns
  • Training Resources: Access to online training videos and discounted local training classes
  • Veterinary Partnerships: Discounted first vet visits with our partner clinics nationwide
  • Behavioral Support: Free consultations with our animal behaviorists for the first 90 days
  • Adoption Alumni Community: Connect with other adopters, share stories, and get advice
  • Pet Insurance Guidance: Help selecting the right insurance plan for your new family member

We're committed to ensuring every adoption is successful for life!
